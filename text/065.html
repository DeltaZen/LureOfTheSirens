“Any surviving pirates will be waiting in ambush, hoping we’re stupid enough to try and board.” You reply. “We’ll leave them to rot.”

“Probably wise.” She says, beginning to plot a course. You glance over at her, but she continues to work the navigation controls. You can feel tension in the air and you’ve been flying with Vanessa long enough to know when she’s biting her tongue.

“Come on, out with it.” You sigh.

“I’m just thinking, it’s a pirate ship.” Vanessa says.

“And pirate ships tend to have holds full of valuables.”

“With just a set of desperate, blood thirsty cutthroats waiting for us.”

“I didn’t say it was without risk.” Vanessa pouts. “It’s up to you… captain.” She adds with more than a hint of insubordination.
