“They attacked me.” You tell Risa firmly. “I was only defending myself. Release the girl or we are going to have a problem.” Your hand goes to your blaster. Around you, twenty blasters are drawn.

“You might have a problem.” Risa smiles. “I won’t, apart from a corpse to dispose of. Either way, the princess is mine.”

“You know how quick on the draw I am.” You tell Risa. “If I die, you die with me.”

“So be it.” Risa shrugs nonchalantly, turning her back to you and wandering away. “But I’d much prefer you to leave it.” She glances over her shoulder. “I’ve been cooped up with these mindless thugs for months now. It would be nice to have a civilised conversation with an old friend.”

“Give me the princess.” You insist.

“No.” She replies. “The princess is mine and that’s final.”
