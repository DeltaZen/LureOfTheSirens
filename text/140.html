“We’ll risk it.” You tell Vanessa.

Seconds pass like hours as the whole cockpit shakes and vibrates as the turbulence increases. You can feel the sweat dripping down your forehead.

“We’re nearly through the nebula.” Vanessa grins, shouting over the noise of the creaking hull. “We’re going to make it!”

You return her smile just as the cockpit suddenly tears open. Your eyes barely have enough time to widen before you are ripped from your seat, dragged through the rent and thrown into the crimson depths of hyperspace where you are instantly and utterly annihilated, your atoms scattered across the universe.
