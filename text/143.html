“Too many.” Vanessa replies. “I wonder what prevented them from leaving.”

“What the fuck?!” You exclaim. “We’re back where we started!”

Vanessa studies the screen in front of her.

“Spatial co-ordinates confirm we are.” She tells you. “But we were on a linear course. We should be far from here.”

“We need to get out of this fucked up nebula.” You reply, dread clawing at you as you begin to fear it is already too late.
