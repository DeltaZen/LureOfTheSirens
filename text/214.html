The tendrils wrap around you, pulling you back. As you struggle to free yourself, more and more wrap around your legs and arms. With a cry of anguish, you are pulled from your feet, more tendrils wrapping around you and dragging you back towards the reactor chamber. You find yourself just a few paces from Vanessa’s blissfully oblivious form, gazing at her powerlessly as you are slowly and painfully drained of life force over the next two days before you finally succumb.
