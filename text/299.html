“Target it.” You tell Vanessa as the strange alien vessel closes in on you.

“Locked on.” Vanessa confirms.

“Fire!” Plasma bolts streak across space, impacting along the shimmering hull. Explosions bloom one after the other before the whole vessel is engulfed in a blinding ball of fire. Red hot debris is thrown in all directions, streaking across space, the destroyed alien ship resembling a huge firework.

“Got the bastard!” Vanessa jumps out of her chair and fist pumps the air.

“We did.” You smile. “Nice shooting.”

“Seemed rather easy in the end.” Vanessa remarks. “Do you think there’s more than one ship?”

“Let’s not wait around to find out.” You tell her, already spooling up the hyperspace engines. “Let’s get the hell out of here.” The Eclipse lurches as the tumbling wrecks of the debris field are replaced by the familiar swirling crimson patterns of hyperspace.
