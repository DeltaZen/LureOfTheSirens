“Take us into hyperspace.” You tell Vanessa. “We don’t want to pick a fight we don’t have to.”

“A tactical retreat eh.” She replies, her fingers playing over the console in front of her. “Course to Omega station locked in.” She adds a moment later, the shimmering alien vessel filling the cockpit window.

“Punch it!” The Eclipse’s hyperspace engines hum quickly builds to a crescendo and the ship lurches as it enters hyperspace. You breathe a deep sigh of relief as you gaze at the swirling crimson patterns of hyperspace as you leave the Dark nebula behind.

“A fortunate escape.” Vanessa comments, puffing out her cheeks, her own relief palpable.

“I told you we were lucky.” You grin nonchalantly. Vanessa shakes her head in disbelief.
